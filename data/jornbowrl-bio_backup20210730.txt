Jian Wang is a research assistanter of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University. Previously, he was a researcher at Xiaomi AI Lab, and worked at 58.Inc, Baidu. Jian Wang received a B.A. in software engineering from Tianjin University, China and an A.I in Computer Vision certified by Tsinghua University. Jian Wang's research interests include machine learning algorithms and applications, adversial methods for privacy data and their applications. His specific interesting is in AI Security Analysis, Web trending Analysis, Privacy Protection. His publications was accepted by ICML2021, Neurpis 2020, and IJCAI 2020.
